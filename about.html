Welcome to Moore Hill Farm & Garden

A family-run North Louisiana farm growing fresh seasonal produce, sharing garden knowledge, and staying connected to the community we serve.

Our Farm

Rooted in family, local soil, and honest work.

Moore Hill Farm & Garden is based in Heflin, Louisiana, where the family grows vegetables on a small scale with care and attention to the season.

The farm is built around fresh harvests, practical growing knowledge, and a simple promise: bring good food to local families whenever the fields are producing.

What We Grow

Seasonal produce for farmers markets and local tables.

The farm focuses on fresh vegetables and seasonal crops, with harvests changing as the weather, soil, and planting schedule shift through the year.

Customers can follow along for weekly availability, market updates, and photos from the fields as different crops come into season.

Sustainable Growing Practices

Growing with care for the land.

Small-scale farming gives the family room to pay close attention to crop health, soil conditions, water needs, and harvest timing.

The goal is steady improvement each season: better beds, better timing, healthier plants, and produce that reflects the work put into the garden.

Community & Education

Sharing the farm beyond the field.

Moore Hill Farm & Garden is part of the local food community, showing up at farmers markets and sharing what is growing throughout the season.

Over time, the farm updates list can become a place for gardening tips, recipes, market reminders, farm news, and community events.
